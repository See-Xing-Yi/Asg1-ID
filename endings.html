Good Ending

Trigger

To obtain the good ending, you must choose not to push winnie off the stairs.

Synopis

Winnie snaps Augustine out of it and tells him that he had “a scary expression” and was sweating a lot, then a sudden talk about the first stairs incident goes and how Winnie knew that Augustine didn't push him, as he tripped himself, but if Augustine wanted to he could had saved him from falling, he stated that was when he seriously reflected on their relationship and why he distanced himseif from Augustine, with fear of destroying their relationship even more, however seeing how silence is ruining it more, Winnie places a hand on Augustine's shoulder and reassures him that he won't be mad or dissapointed, but to tell him everything, then everything that was bottling up starts to spill out. Reasonings, explanations and apologies were shared between the two, and like the first few cgs of the game, they share a fist bump, now then shifting topics from the coma dream, the elkbear, and how to get out of the dream, they realize the items they have in hand, a gas and a lighter. Now knowing what they will do, they went downstairs and waited til' the monster shows up, when it did, Winnie douses the monster with gas and Augustine lights it up on fire. They share a hug, but after not knowing what to do after this, they burn in the same fire that the abomination burned in.

The tone of the scene here is blue, Augustine wakes up and shouts out Winnie's name. Seeing how Winnie is smiling, Augustine says how can he smile at a time like this, but Winnie says how Augustine is smiling aswell. In the hospital, it is stated that Winnie had minor injuries but was fine, on the other hand, Augustine broke his leg, due to the incident, Winnie's move was pushed farther, Winnie stayed by Augustine's side the entire time making sure he'll heal. Eventually, Winnie left. However despite that, it doesn't mean that Augustine will be alone.

Bad Ending

Trigger

To obtain the bad ending, you must choose to push winnie off the stairs.

Synopis

As Augustine pushes Winnie off the stairs, Winnie turns back to look at him for the last time. A marbled Augustine smiles for a brief moment before revealing Winnie's dead body. Augustine wakes up from the coma dream and refers to Winnie as "He" instead of his name, after seeing Winnie's body and seeing that he isn't breathing, he smiles. Back from the hospital, everyone starts to pay attention to him because Winnie is gone, despite it being implied to some that it was only out of pure pity, Augustine smiles again and says that he's finally not alone.
