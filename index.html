Augustine and Winnie, lifelong friends since elementary school, navigate the challenges of separation as Winnie, having been accepted to university, prepares to leave their hometown. Eager to spend their final day together before Winnie's departure, they set out in Winnie's car. However, an unexpected twist unfolds as they find themselves caught in a freezing blizzard in the summer month of July.
