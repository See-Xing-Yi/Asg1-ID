Winnie Bosko

Nationality - Canadian

Gender - Male

Age - 18

Date of Birth - December 21

Personality

As a child, Winnie was shown to be incredibly shy, timid, and wary of others and their actions, particularly when a younger Augustine approached him. Nevertheless, with Augustine's words of encouragement, Winnie drew inspiration from Augustine's attitude, gradually adopting similar traits during his early years.

As Winnie grew older, he displayed obvious similarities to the younger Augustine - bubbly, cheerful and optimistic. Winnie often approached situations with a lighthearted perspective, not taking them as seriously as Augustine did, as seen when he built a snowman during a blizzard while Augustine focused on looking for useful items in the car. Despite this carefree attitude, Winnie demonstrates a keen analytical ability, making deductions about their surroundings, such as sudden clothing changes and peculiar settings. Additionally, Winnie consistently prioritized Augustine's needs over his own, refusing to burden Augustine when offered to carry him due to Winnie being unable to handle the cold of the blizzard. As the narrative unfolds towards the conclusion in the good route, Winnie is shown to be very understanding of Augustine, and open minded to what he tells him.

Though not included in the game, the creator has stated that Winnie doesn't understand boundaries, he would make jokes or share information about Augustine that he would feel too personal about, he doesn't do it on purpose as he simply believe that is just what close friends do, not realising how he had hurt him.

Relations with Augustine

Winnie's relationship with Augustine began in childhood when Winnie, having recently moved into the house adjacent to Augustine's residence, faced the challenge of making new friends. Augustine's mother, noticing Winnie's hesitancy, suggested that Augustine approach him. Despite initial resistance, Winnie eventually opened up, expressing the difficulties of leaving friends behind during the move. Augustine in response, offered encouragement, fostering a friendship that became inseparable over the years.

However, their bond faced a strain when Winnie fell down a flight of stairs one winter, breaking his leg in the process. Winnie notes how he knew Augustine hadn't been the one who caused Winnie to fall down the stairs but at the time, Augustine was at a distance near Winnie where if Augustine wanted to, he would have been able to reach out and stop him from falling. It also turns out, that as Winnie was passing out from the fall, someone else had called for help for Winnie besides Augustine, as he had left the scene already. The two ceased communication for a long while, holding mutual misunderstandings as Winnie had thought Augustine hated him.

Later on, Augustine, plagued by severe insecurity and jealousy issues, particularly intensified when Winnie had gotten accepted into a university he also applied for but failed to get accepted into. As Winnie prepared to move away for university, he and Augustine opted to spend Winnie's last days together on a car ride to somewhere unspecified. However, an unexpected argument ensued due to Augustine's hallucinations of Winnie being extremely hostile, resulting in a car crash and the onset of shared flashbacks.
